The Hands Behind the Work

We're a local Greenville handyman service built on hard work, honest pricing, and a genuine love for helping our neighbors improve their homes.

Our Journey

Built One Project at a Time

2015

Where It All Started

What began as one man with a toolbox and a pickup truck quickly turned into something bigger. We started taking on small repair jobs around Greenville — fixing leaky faucets, patching drywall, and helping neighbors with projects they couldn't tackle alone.

2017

Expanded Our Services

Demand grew and so did our capabilities. We brought on skilled tradespeople and expanded into kitchen remodels, bathroom renovations, flooring installation, and electrical work — becoming a true full-service handyman operation.

2020

500+ Projects Completed

Even through challenging times, Greenville homeowners trusted us with their repairs and renovations. Hitting this milestone proved that quality work and honest communication speak louder than any ad.

2023

Serving the Entire Upstate

Today we proudly serve Greenville, Taylors, Simpsonville, Greer, Mauldin, and the greater Upstate region. Our team has grown, but our promise hasn't changed — treat every home like our own.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Honesty

Transparent pricing with no hidden fees. We tell you exactly what the job requires and what it will cost before we pick up a single tool. If we think you don't need the work, we'll say so.

Quality

We do it right the first time. Every repair, every renovation, every installation meets our high standard. We'd rather take extra time than cut a corner — and we stand behind every project with a satisfaction guarantee.

Community

We live and work right here in Greenville. Supporting local businesses, hiring local talent, and investing in the neighborhoods we serve isn't just a talking point — it's how we operate every day.
