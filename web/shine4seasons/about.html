Our Beginning

A Passion for Beauty

Conveniently situated at 2017 Wade Hampton Blvd C in Greenville, SC 29615, Shine 4 Seasons Salon was created as an ideal retreat for clients seeking a luxurious atmosphere without the luxury price tag.

Our name reflects our philosophy — beauty isn't seasonal, it's year-round. Whether it's spring nails blooming with color, summer toes ready for sandals, autumn tones for a cozy look, or winter self-care to stay pampered — we're here for every chapter.

"We deliver top-notch services at reasonable prices — because luxury should be accessible to everyone."
