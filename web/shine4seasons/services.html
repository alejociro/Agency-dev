FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are always welcome! However, we recommend booking online for guaranteed availability, especially on weekends. You can book anytime at our online booking portal.

What hygiene measures do you follow?

Every pedicure includes a personal spa protective liner. We use single-use pumice bars, nail files, and buffer sets. All tools are sanitized between clients.

Do you offer services for kids?

Yes! We have dedicated kid services with gentle, fun nail treatments. It's a great parent-child activity. Bring the whole family!

Do you sell gift certificates?

Yes, gift certificates are available in any amount. Visit the salon or call us at 864-203-3947 to purchase. The perfect gift for any occasion!

What about facial appointment deposits?

Facial appointments require a deposit sent via Zelle to Shine4SeasonsSalon@gmail.com. This secures your time slot and is applied to your service total.
