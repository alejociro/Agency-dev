Welcome to Indigo

A space where creativity
and vitality bloom together

Indigo Flow and Art combines Classical Pilates and Fine Art into a singular studio in the heart of West Greenville's Village. Step through our old French doors into a light-filled Pilates room with a fully equipped Gratz studio, or linger in the vibrant gallery of co-owner and local artist Julie Hughes.

Classical Pilates

Precision in
Every Movement

Indigo Pilates is a Classical Pilates studio instructing students in Joseph Pilates' original Contrology Method. Every session begins with the fundamentals of connection — building strength, alignment, and body awareness from the inside out.

Fine Art Gallery

The Everyday,
Made Ephemeral

Through her work, Julie Hughes strives to combine the everyday with the ephemeral — capturing real moments viewed through a surreal lens. Her recent works explore motion, speed, and gravity-defying angles, particularly in the art of bicycle racing.
