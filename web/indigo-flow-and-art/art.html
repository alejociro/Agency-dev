The Artist

Julie Hughes

“Through my work, I strive to combine the everyday with the ephemeral. To capture real moments that are viewed from a surreal lens.”

Julie's themes are not concerned with what the world should be, but rather with what the world is — while choosing to see reality with slight distortions. She strives to communicate the essence, power, and movement of a scene or a person.

Her recent works are all about motion, specifically motion as we see it in the art of bicycle racing. It's all about speed, motion, and gravity-defying angles, and how such intangible things can be put on canvas.
