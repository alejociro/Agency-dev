Common Questions

Good to know

Do I need an appointment or do you accept walk-ins?
We welcome both! While walk-ins are accepted, we recommend booking online through Vagaro to guarantee your preferred stylist and time slot.
What is the Scalp Lounge?
The Capello Scalp Lounge offers targeted solutions for scalp health, thinning hair, and hair loss through advanced trichology-based treatments. Think of it as a spa for your scalp — luxurious head spa treatments, scalp facials, and therapeutic massages.
How long does a color appointment take?
Color services typically range from 2 to 4 hours depending on the complexity. Balayage and foilayage services may take longer for first-time clients. Your stylist will give you a time estimate during your consultation.
Do you offer gift certificates?
Yes! Gift certificates are available for any amount and can be purchased online or at the salon. They make a perfect gift for any occasion.
What's your cancellation policy?
We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. Please check our policies page for full details.
Do you host parties and events?
Absolutely! We offer party bookings for bridal showers, birthdays, girls' nights, and more. Contact us for availability and custom packages.
Where are your locations?
We have two locations: our flagship salon at 102 Mills Ave in Downtown Greenville, SC, and a second location in Travelers Rest, SC. Both offer the full range of hair services and the Scalp Lounge.
Are you hiring?
We're always looking for talented stylists to join our team. We also offer an apprentice program for aspiring professionals. Contact us to learn more about career opportunities.
