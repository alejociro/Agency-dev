Our Story

Why They Call Us
"After Hours"

When we started in 1996, we noticed something: plumbing emergencies don't wait for business hours. Pipes burst at midnight. Water heaters fail on holidays. That's why we built our business around being there when others aren't.

The name stuck — and so did our commitment. Three decades later, we're still the team Wenatchee Valley calls when something goes wrong, no matter what the clock says.

Now offering full electrical services, we're your one-call solution for everything that keeps your home running.
