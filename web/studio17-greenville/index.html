Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need experience to start?

Absolutely not. Classical Pilates is designed for every body and every level. BeLinda will adapt every exercise to your current ability and build you up progressively. Many clients start with zero experience.

What should I wear to a session?

Wear comfortable, form-fitting clothing that allows you to move freely. Avoid loose shirts or baggy pants that can get caught in the equipment. Grip socks are recommended but not required — bare feet work well too.

What is the difference between Classical and contemporary Pilates?

Classical Pilates follows the original order and exercises created by Joseph Pilates. It uses the full system of apparatus (Reformer, Cadillac, Chair, Barrels) in a specific sequence designed to systematically work the entire body. Contemporary styles often modify or omit these foundational principles.

How often should I practice?

Joseph Pilates recommended practicing three to four times per week. However, even one or two sessions per week can produce meaningful results. Consistency matters more than frequency.

Can Pilates help with back pain?

Many of our clients come specifically for back pain relief. Pilates strengthens the deep stabilizing muscles of the core and spine, improving posture and reducing strain. Always consult your physician first if you have a specific injury or condition.
