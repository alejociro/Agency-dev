Contrology by Balanced Body

Professional Apparatus

Studio 17 is equipped with the gold standard in Classical Pilates equipment. Every piece is professional-grade Contrology by Balanced Body — designed to honor Joseph Pilates' original specifications.

Reformer

The cornerstone of the Pilates system. Spring-loaded resistance on a sliding carriage for hundreds of exercises targeting every muscle group.

Cadillac

The most versatile piece of Pilates apparatus. A full trapeze table with springs, bars, and straps for deep stretching and strengthening work.

Wunda Chair

Compact and challenging. The Chair builds exceptional balance, coordination, and deep stabilizer strength that transfers directly to daily movement.

Ladder Barrel

Designed for deep spinal articulation and flexibility. The curved surface supports your body through stretches and strengthening exercises impossible on a flat surface.

Spine Corrector

As the name suggests — it corrects spinal alignment. Perfect for opening the chest, improving posture, and developing back extension strength.

Mat Work

The foundation of the method. Joseph Pilates' original 34 mat exercises develop core control, flexibility, and body awareness using only your own resistance.
