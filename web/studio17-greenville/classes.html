Contrology by Balanced Body

Professional Apparatus

Studio 17 is equipped with the gold standard in Classical Pilates equipment. Every piece is professional-grade Contrology by Balanced Body — designed to honor Joseph Pilates' original specifications.

Click any piece to learn more about how it’s used in your practice.

Reformer

The cornerstone of the Pilates system. Spring-loaded resistance on a sliding carriage for hundreds of exercises.

Used in both private sessions and group classes. Five spring settings allow precise resistance control from rehabilitation-gentle to athlete-challenging. The carriage glides silently on professional Balanced Body rails.

Cadillac

The most versatile piece of Pilates apparatus. A full trapeze table with springs, bars, and straps.

Primarily used in private sessions. The overhead frame allows suspended exercises impossible on other equipment. Ideal for deep spinal decompression and advanced hanging work.

Wunda Chair

Compact and challenging. Builds exceptional balance, coordination, and deep stabilizer strength.

Don’t let the size fool you — the Chair is one of the most demanding pieces in the studio. Standing exercises on the Chair develop balance that translates directly to real-life movement.

Ladder Barrel

Designed for deep spinal articulation and flexibility through its unique curved surface.

The barrel’s curve matches the natural arc of the spine, making it uniquely effective for back extension, side bending, and hamstring stretches that no flat surface can replicate.

Spine Corrector

Corrects spinal alignment. Perfect for opening the chest and developing back extension.

A favorite among desk workers and clients with rounded posture. The Spine Corrector gently opens the thoracic spine and strengthens the muscles that hold you upright throughout the day.

Mat Work

The foundation: Joseph Pilates’ original 34 exercises using only your own body resistance.

Mat work is where every Classical Pilates journey begins and where mastery is ultimately tested. No springs, no assistance — just you and the method. The goal of all apparatus work is to improve your mat practice.
