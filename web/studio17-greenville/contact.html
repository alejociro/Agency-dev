Before You Visit

Good to Know

What should I expect at my first session?

Your first session begins with a brief conversation about your goals, movement history, and any injuries or conditions. Then BeLinda will guide you through a tailored introduction to the Classical method on the Reformer and other apparatus. Wear comfortable, form-fitting clothes. No prior experience needed.

How do I book a session?

Use the contact form above, email us at info@studio17greenville.com, or reach out via Instagram DM @studio17greenvillesc. BeLinda will respond within 24 hours to confirm your appointment.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the spot to another client who may be waiting. Late cancellations may be subject to the full session fee.
