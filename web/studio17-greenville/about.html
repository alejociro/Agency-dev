17 About Studio 17

Where Discipline
Meets Grace

Studio 17 was born from a belief that Classical Pilates — practiced with intention and precision — can transform not just bodies, but lives.

The Founder

BeLinda

My journey to Classical Pilates wasn't a straight line. Like many of my clients, I discovered the method when my body needed something different — something more precise, more intentional than what conventional fitness offered.

What I found in the Classical method was a complete system. Not just exercises, but a philosophy of movement that respects the body's design and works with it, not against it. I trained under master teachers in the lineage of Joseph Pilates, and I bring that depth of knowledge to every session.

At Studio 17, I don't follow trends. I follow the method — because after more than a century, it still works better than anything else.

Our Philosophy

What We Believe

Precision Over Repetition

Ten perfect movements are more valuable than a hundred sloppy ones. Every exercise in the Classical method has a specific purpose, a precise form, and a clear intention. We honor that precision in every session.

The Whole Body, Every Time

Joseph Pilates designed his system to work the entire body in every session — not isolated muscle groups. That's why our clients leave feeling balanced, energized, and aligned, not just sore in one area.

Personal Attention Always

Even in group classes, you are never anonymous. BeLinda sees every body, corrects every alignment, and adapts every exercise. Your practice is always yours, even when shared with others.

The Method Works

We don't add gimmicks, trendy modifications, or playlist-driven "vibes" to the practice. Classical Pilates has been transforming bodies for over a century because the system is complete. We trust the method.

The Journey

How Studio 17 Came to Be

The Beginning

Discovering Classical Pilates

BeLinda discovered the Classical method while recovering from a chronic injury. The precision and intelligence of Joseph Pilates' original system changed her relationship with her body entirely.

Training

Studying Under Master Teachers

Committed to learning the method at its deepest level, BeLinda pursued comprehensive training in the Classical Pilates lineage — learning not just exercises, but the philosophy, the sequencing, and the art of teaching bodies to move.

The Vision

Opening Studio 17

Studio 17 was created to bring authentic Classical Pilates to Greenville. A space equipped with professional Contrology by Balanced Body apparatus, where the method could be practiced as Joseph Pilates intended — with care, with precision, and with personal attention.

Today

Growing the Community

Every week, new clients discover what Classical Pilates can do. From athletes to desk workers, from post-rehab to pure wellness — Studio 17 continues to grow because the method continues to transform.
