Our Story

Where Artistry Meets Purity

A Perfect 10 Nails was founded with one simple belief: every client deserves both stunning nail art and uncompromising hygiene. Located in the heart of Greenville, SC, we've built our reputation on the promise that beauty should never come at the cost of safety.

Our team of skilled nail technicians brings years of experience and a genuine passion for the craft. From classic manicures to elaborate nail art, every service is performed with fresh, sterilized tools and disposable liners — because your health is as important as your style.

We proudly serve ladies, gentlemen, and kids of all ages. Walk-ins are always welcome, but booking ahead guarantees your preferred time and technician.
