Our Story

Since 1990, the Watson
Family Has Delivered

Greenville Process Service was founded in 1990 by the Watson family with a simple mission: provide honest, dependable process service for the legal professionals of the Upstate. What began as a small family operation has grown into the trusted name that law firms across five counties rely on.

Today, Pat Watson, Travis Watson, and Jim Watson continue to run the business with the same values it was built on — integrity, reliability, and a personal touch that you won't find at a large corporation. When you call Greenville Process Service, you talk to an owner, not an answering service.

Over 36 years, we've built relationships with courthouse clerks, law firms, and legal professionals throughout Greenville, Anderson, Pickens, Oconee, and Spartanburg counties. That institutional knowledge is something no newcomer can replicate — it's what makes the difference between a document served on time and a case delayed.
