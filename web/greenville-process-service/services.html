Core Service

Service of Process

Service of process is the legal procedure of delivering court documents to a party involved in a legal action. It ensures that every individual receives proper notice of legal proceedings — a constitutional right that demands precision and compliance.

At Greenville Process Service, we specialize in serving the documents that keep the legal system moving across the Upstate of South Carolina. From routine filings to complex cases, our team handles every document with the same level of care and urgency.

Investigative Service

Skip Trace

When a defendant can't be located at their known address, you need a skip trace. Our service tracks down individuals who have moved, are evading service, or whose contact information is outdated.

A failed service attempt costs time and money. Our skip trace minimizes non-service costs by locating defendants before additional attempts are wasted. We use multiple data sources and our deep knowledge of the Upstate region to find people that others can't.

Skip trace services are available upon request and can be combined with process service for a seamless workflow from location to delivery.

Request Skip Trace →

When You Need Skip Trace

  • Defendant not found at last known address
  • Multiple failed service attempts
  • Outdated or incomplete contact information
  • Defendant suspected of evading service
