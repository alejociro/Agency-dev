Our Story

From Neighbor to Your
Most Trusted Handyman

Around The House Service was born from a simple belief: every homeowner deserves a handyman they can trust. Based in Mauldin, South Carolina, we started as a one-person operation helping neighbors with home repairs — and that personal touch is still at the core of everything we do.

As a licensed and insured service provider, we bring professional standards to every job, whether it's a quick faucet replacement or a full deck build. We serve both residential and commercial customers across upstate South Carolina, and we treat every project — large or small — with the same care we'd give our own home.

Today, with 99 five-star Google reviews, our reputation speaks for itself. But we're not resting on it. Every new job is a chance to prove why our neighbors keep calling us back.
