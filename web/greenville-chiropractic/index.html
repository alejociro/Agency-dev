What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive care tailored to your body, backed by 50+ years of clinical experience.

Spinal Adjustment

Precise, gentle adjustments that restore proper alignment, relieve nerve pressure, and help your body heal naturally. Our doctors tailor each adjustment to your unique condition.

Spinal Decompression

Non-surgical treatment for herniated discs, sciatica, and chronic back pain. Gentle stretching creates negative pressure that helps bulging discs retract and heal.

On-Premise X-Ray

No need for separate imaging appointments. Our in-office X-ray services give us an immediate, detailed look at your spine so we can diagnose accurately and start treatment faster.

Massage Therapy

Therapeutic massage works alongside chiropractic care to release muscle tension, improve circulation, and accelerate your recovery. The perfect complement to spinal adjustment.

Your first visit begins with a thorough evaluation by Dr. DeKraker or Dr. Cushman. They'll review your health history, perform a physical exam, and may take X-rays in our on-site facility. Then they'll explain their findings and recommend a personalized care plan. The entire process typically takes about 45 minutes.

We accept most major insurance plans. We recommend calling our office at (616) 754-9172 to verify your specific coverage before your visit. We also offer flexible payment options for patients without insurance or with high deductibles.

After your initial evaluation, regular adjustment visits typically take 15-20 minutes. If your care plan includes massage therapy, please allow additional time. We respect your schedule and work hard to stay on time.
