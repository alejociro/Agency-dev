Our Dental Services

Everything Your Family Needs Under One Roof

Choice One Dental Care provides a host of dental services to the entire family, making us Georgia and South Carolina's premier family dentistry provider.

Family Dentistry

Comprehensive dental care for every member of your family, from toddlers to grandparents. We create a welcoming environment for all ages.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with whitening, veneers, and bonding. Receive the cosmetic treatment you need to feel confident again.

Preventative Dentistry

Stay ahead of dental issues with regular cleanings, exams, and screenings. We help you maintain optimal oral health for life.

Restorative Dentistry

From crowns and bridges to fillings and implants — we restore your teeth to full function and natural beauty.

Orthodontics

Straighten teeth and correct bite issues for teens and adults. If your teenager requires orthodontics, you can get them here.

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, kid-friendly dental care that makes children feel safe and comfortable. Building healthy habits from the start.

Sedation Dentistry

Nervous about the dentist? We go to great lengths to make anxious patients comfortable with gentle sedation options for stress-free visits.
