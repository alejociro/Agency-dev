Your Doctors

Lead Dentist

Dr. Trey Kenna, DMD, FAGD

Dr. Kenna is a Fellow of the Academy of General Dentistry (FAGD) — a credential earned through over 500 hours of continuing education and rigorous examinations. This commitment to lifelong learning means your care is informed by the latest techniques and technology in dentistry.

He specializes in implant placement and restoration, bone grafting, oral surgery, and IV sedation — all performed in-house. His philosophy is simple: listen first, explain thoroughly, and never rush a patient.

Implant Specialist IV Sedation Certified FAGD Fellow
Dentist

Dr. Martin, DMD

Dr. Martin brings a compassionate, patient-first approach to every procedure. With expertise across general, cosmetic, and family dentistry, she ensures every member of your family receives personalized, thorough care.

Together with Dr. Kenna, the practice covers the full spectrum of dental services — from routine cleanings to complex surgical cases — without the need for outside referrals.
