Our Services

A Full Range of Dental Care

Our practice features a full range of affordable dental services to care for your smile, powered by the latest technology.

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening treatments that brighten your smile safely and effectively, with results you can see in a single visit.

Custom Porcelain Veneers

Thin, custom-made shells bonded to the front of teeth to correct chips, stains, gaps, and misalignment for a flawless appearance.

Dental Bonding

Tooth-colored composite resin applied to repair chipped, cracked, or discolored teeth, restoring a natural look in one appointment.

Invisible Braces

Clear aligner therapy to straighten teeth discreetly, without the look of traditional metal braces.

Tooth-Colored Fillings

Natural-looking composite fillings that blend seamlessly with your teeth, replacing old silver amalgam fillings.

Routine Cleanings & Exams

Comprehensive dental cleanings and thorough examinations to maintain your oral health and catch issues early.

Digital X-Rays

Low-radiation digital imaging for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning, with instant results you can view on screen.

Fluoride Treatments

Protective fluoride applications to strengthen tooth enamel and prevent cavities, especially important for children.

Sealants

Protective coatings applied to the chewing surfaces of back teeth to prevent decay in hard-to-reach grooves.

Crowns

Custom-crafted dental crowns that restore damaged teeth to their original shape, strength, and appearance.

Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacement solutions anchored directly into the jawbone, looking and functioning like natural teeth.

Fixed Bridges

Non-removable dental bridges that span the gap left by missing teeth, anchored to adjacent healthy teeth.

Inlays & Onlays

Conservative restorations for teeth with moderate damage that do not require a full crown.

Dentures & Partials

Custom-fit full and partial dentures to restore your complete smile and chewing ability with comfort and confidence.

Root Canals

Gentle root canal therapy to save infected teeth and relieve pain, preserving your natural tooth structure.

Non-Surgical Periodontal Therapy

Deep cleaning and scaling to treat gum disease without surgery, helping restore gum health.

Tooth Removal

Safe, comfortable tooth extractions when a tooth cannot be saved, with options for replacement afterward.

Sedation Dentistry

Sedation options for anxious patients to ensure a calm, comfortable dental experience without stress.

Night Guards

Custom-fitted night guards to protect teeth from grinding and clenching during sleep.

Advanced Technology

CEREC Same-Day Restorations

Chairside Economical Restoration of Esthetic Ceramic (CEREC) technology uses digital modeling and CAD/CAM dentistry to create affordable ceramic restorations right in our office.

What previously required multiple visits and temporary crowns can now be completed in a single appointment. Digital impressions mean no more messy molds.

  • Same-day crowns and restorations
  • Digital impressions (no messy molds)
  • Precise CAD/CAM fabrication
  • Natural-looking ceramic material
