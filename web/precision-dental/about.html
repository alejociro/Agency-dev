About Precision Dental

Smiles Without Barriers

A dental practice built on the belief that everyone deserves quality care, regardless of their background.

Our Vision

We dream of a day when every Dentist will have a diverse team, accept a diverse clientele, and be willing to see those patients that are often rejected.

We dream of becoming a nationally recognized Black-owned dental practice known for welcoming underrepresented communities and removing barriers to multilingual care.

Our Mission

Creating Spaces That Welcome Everyone

Our mission is to create spaces to encourage diversity and welcome an ever-changing society. We position ourselves as approachable practitioners committed to reducing dental anxiety.

We believe comfortable environments are essential. When patients feel welcome and understood, they are more likely to prioritize their oral health and maintain regular visits.

Meet Our Founder

Dr. Tywana M Groce, DMD

Dr. Groce pursued dentistry after learning only 3% of dentists are people of color. She completed her education at Hampton University and her DDS at Temple University School of Dentistry.

She spent 3 years in public health before establishing private practice in the Upstate South Carolina area, where she has practiced for nearly 15 years.
