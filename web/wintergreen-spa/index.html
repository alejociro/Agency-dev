Before Your Visit

What should I expect on my first visit?

Arrive 10 minutes early to complete a brief intake form. Your therapist will discuss your goals, areas of concern, and any health considerations before beginning your treatment. The studio offers a calm, private environment designed for your comfort.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes, all services are by appointment only. You can book by calling (864) 351-8209 or using the contact form on our website. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance.

What forms of payment do you accept?

For in-spa payments and tips, we accept cash or checks only. Gift certificates can be purchased online with a credit card through our website shop.

What are the benefits of the Salt Room?

Halotherapy (salt therapy) helps with bronchial inflammation, removes airborne pollen from airways, strengthens the immune system, and helps prevent and treat common cold, flu, allergies, asthma, sinusitis, and sinus headaches. Sessions are $25 each.

What are your hours?

Monday through Friday: 9am - 6pm. Saturday: 8am - 4pm. Sunday: 11am - 4pm. All visits are by appointment only.
