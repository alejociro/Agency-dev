What We Do

We Specialize in
Improving Smiles

A comprehensive selection of treatment options in a comfortable, convenient atmosphere.

General Dentistry

From routine cleanings and exams to comprehensive oral health evaluations, we help you preserve your natural teeth and maintain long-term wellness. Dentistry encompasses an array of services with one common goal: keeping you healthy and smiling.

Book a Checkup

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening that restores your brightest smile. Safe, effective procedures for stained or discolored teeth.

Dental Implants

A permanent solution for missing teeth. Modern implants that look, feel, and function like your natural teeth.

Crowns & Bridges

Restore damaged or decayed teeth with custom-fitted crowns. Durable, natural-looking restorations.

Preventive Care

Regular exams, cleanings, and early detection keep small issues from becoming big problems.

Periodontal Exams & Fillings

Comprehensive gum health assessments and modern fillings to restore tooth structure. We use the latest materials for durability and a natural appearance.

Answers Before
You Visit

Everything new patients want to know.

Your first visit includes a comprehensive oral health evaluation, X-rays if needed, and a consultation with Dr. Fletcher. We take time to understand your dental history, concerns, and goals before recommending any treatment. Expect a comfortable, no-pressure experience.

We accept most major dental insurance plans. Our front office team is happy to verify your coverage before your appointment. For patients without insurance, our VIP Membership Club provides significant savings on preventive care and treatments.
