What We Do

Professional Home Repair Services

From small fixes to full room makeovers, Ryan personally handles every job with the care and precision your home deserves.

Carpentry

I offer a wide range of carpentry services, including framing, trim work, custom cabinetry, and custom furniture. Whether it's a built-in bookshelf or crown molding, every piece is crafted to last.

Painting

I provide interior painting services, including prep work, priming, and painting with high-quality paints. Clean lines, even coats, and a finish that transforms any room.

Plumbing

Is your toilet running? I can help with repair and maintenance of faucets, toilets, showers, and other plumbing fixtures. Quick fixes that save you from bigger problems down the road.

Electrical

I can help with minor electrical needs, from installing light fixtures and ceiling fans to troubleshooting minor electrical problems. Safe, code-compliant work every time.

Drywall & General Repairs

Have a small drywall repair? A squeaky door? A loose railing? We can handle all types of general repairs, from fixing leaky faucets to repairing damaged walls and floors. No job is too small.

Furniture Repair & Assembly

Have a piece of nice furniture that sustained some damage? Did you bring home a car full of boxes from Ikea? I can repair, restore, or assemble your furniture quickly and properly.

Vacation & Airbnb Maintenance

Have a vacation home or Airbnb in the area? I'll get it back in tip top shape for your next guests. Regular maintenance keeps your property looking its best and reviews positive.

About ATH Home Services

Meet Ryan — The Handyman Who Actually Shows Up

Around The House Home Handyman Services is a family-owned and operated business with over 30 years of experience in the residential repair, remodeling, and construction industry.

At Around The House we focus on getting the small jobs done in a reliable and professional manner. Ryan performs all on-site work personally, using quality materials and tools. He offers flexible scheduling accommodating evenings and weekends, and provides upfront estimates with competitive pricing.

If a project falls outside his expertise, Ryan will let you know honestly and point you in the right direction with a trusted referral. That's the kind of transparency that's built this business for three decades.
