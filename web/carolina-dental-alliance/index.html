About Us

Compassionate Dental Care Is Right Around The Corner

From Upstate South Carolina to the Coast, the friendly dentists and dental professionals at Carolina Dental Alliance practices are dedicated to providing you with exceptional dental care in a relaxed, comfortable atmosphere.

We love to see those beautiful Carolina smiles, so we make it easy for you to get the dental care you need to keep your smile brighter, healthier and happier for a lifetime.

No matter which of our 17 practices you visit, you will find a caring team of professionals, equipped with the latest in dental technology, ready to care for your dental needs. They will answer any questions, explain all of your options and provide the information you need to be better informed and more relaxed.

All of the dental professionals at Carolina Dental Alliance maintain the highest levels of accreditation and pursue ongoing education to stay abreast of the latest trends in dentistry.

To make your visit even easier, we accept and file most traditional insurance and even have payment options.
