Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What areas in Greenville do you serve?
We serve all of Greenville, SC and the surrounding upstate area, including Simpsonville, Mauldin, Greer, Taylors, and Travelers Rest. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, just give us a call.
What cleaning products do you use?
We use professional-grade, eco-friendly cleaning products that are safe for children, pets, and the environment. If you have specific product preferences or allergies, let us know and we'll accommodate.
How much does a cleaning session cost?
Pricing depends on the size of your space, the type of cleaning, and the frequency. We provide free estimates with no obligations — just reach out through our contact form or call us at (864) 438-0458.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
Not at all. Many of our clients provide access instructions and go about their day. We're fully insured and all team members are background-checked for your peace of mind.
What's your cancellation policy?
We ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Last-minute changes happen — just give us a call and we'll do our best to accommodate.
Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?
Yes! We arrive fully equipped with all cleaning supplies, tools, and equipment. You don't need to provide anything. If you prefer we use specific products you own, just let us know.
Do you offer gift cards?
Yes! We offer cleaning gift cards in any amount. They make a great gift for housewarmings, birthdays, holidays, or anyone who could use a break. Contact us to purchase.
