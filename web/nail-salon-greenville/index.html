What We Cover

Popular Nail Services

From timeless classics to the latest trends — find exactly the service you're looking for.

Classic Manicure

The foundation of nail care. Trimming, shaping, cuticle care, and polish in a relaxing setting. Perfect for weekly maintenance.

Gel Polish

Long-lasting, chip-resistant color that shines for 2-3 weeks. UV-cured for a flawless, glossy finish every time.

Acrylic Extensions

Add length and strength with sculpted acrylic nails. Customizable shapes from almond to coffin, with endless design options.

Nail Art & Design

Express yourself with hand-painted designs, chrome finishes, ombré gradients, and 3D embellishments by skilled artists.

Spa Pedicure

A full pampering experience for your feet — soak, exfoliation, massage, and polish in a soothing environment.

Dip Powder

No UV light needed. Dip powder delivers rich, even color with added strength. Lasts up to 4 weeks with zero chipping.
