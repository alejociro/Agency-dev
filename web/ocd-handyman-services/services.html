FAQ

Common Questions

What areas do you serve?

We serve the greater Greenville, SC area including Taylors, Mauldin, Simpsonville, Greer, and surrounding communities within Greenville County.

How do I get a free estimate?

Call us at 864-283-6201 or fill out the contact form on our website. We'll schedule a free on-site visit, assess your project, and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. OCD Handyman Services is fully insured to do business in South Carolina. For projects requiring licensed plumbers or electricians, we have licensed professionals on call.

How long does a typical renovation take?

Timelines vary by project scope. A bathroom remodel typically takes 2-3 weeks, kitchen remodels 3-6 weeks, and smaller repairs can often be completed in 1-3 days. We'll provide a specific timeline with your estimate.

Do you offer a warranty on your work?

Yes. Our warranty varies by project type and will be discussed in detail before acceptance of any job. We stand behind our work and want you completely satisfied.

Do you supply the materials?

Yes. We have established accounts with local suppliers for premium materials at competitive prices. We pass these savings on to you. You're also welcome to supply your own materials if you prefer.
