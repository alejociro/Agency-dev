Our Story

Property Managers
Turned Problem Solvers

Greenville Maintenance Services, Inc. was founded in 1993 by Tommy Jenkins and Rex Jones — two commercial property managers who were tired of unreliable contractors and inconsistent service.

Having managed commercial properties themselves, they understood what property managers actually need: a single, trustworthy partner who shows up on schedule, catches problems early, and communicates clearly.

Over three decades later, that founding vision hasn't changed. We provide full maintenance services for commercial properties including sweeping, parking lot maintenance, building and grounds maintenance, and general contracting — all backed by over 75 combined years of property management and construction experience.
