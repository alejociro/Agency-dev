FAQ

Common Questions

What areas do you serve?
We serve Upstate South Carolina (Greenville, Spartanburg, Anderson), western North Carolina (Asheville area), and northeastern Georgia. Our Greenville headquarters allows us to efficiently cover the entire tri-state region.
How often should my parking lot be swept?
It depends on traffic volume and property type. High-traffic retail properties typically need nightly sweeping, while office parks may only need weekly service. We'll assess your property and recommend the right schedule.
Do you handle emergency or after-hours requests?
Yes. We understand that maintenance issues don't follow a 9-to-5 schedule. Contact us for urgent needs and we'll coordinate a response as quickly as possible.
How long does sealcoating last?
A properly applied sealcoat typically lasts 2-3 years depending on traffic, weather, and existing pavement condition. We recommend reassessment every 2 years to maintain optimal protection.
Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. We carry full commercial liability insurance and all required licenses for work in South Carolina, North Carolina, and Georgia. Certificates of insurance are available upon request.
